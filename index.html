01. Overview

There are many versions of the same moment, but only one is allowed to remain. The phenomenon called PRIME is not a person or an object. It is the end result of conflict between overlapping realities.

People begin normally, unaware of the discrepancies around them. Memories shift without warning. Objects appear in states they were never in. Conversations include lines that were never spoken.

This illusion persists until the weight of contradictions becomes too large to sustain. This is where PRIME begins to form.

02. Phase Structure

2.1 Phase 1 — Start of Story

Phase 1 resembles a stable world. People continue through familiar routines, unaware that their timeline is being subtly edited. Small differences are rationalized automatically. Rooms shift layout, timestamps adjust, messages appear out of order.

These are not random errors. They are pressure from other branches attempting to overwrite this one.

2.2 Phase 2 — Strange Occurrences

Screams and voices echo from empty rooms. Gunshot sounds loop too perfectly. Digital images distort slightly. Messages reappear after deletion.

Fragments of information leak through: symbols, cryptic warnings, inconsistent screenshots. None reveal the complete truth, but all destabilize confidence in the current timeline.

2.3 Phase 3 — Breaking Point

Contradictions overwhelm the subject. Images and logs conflict with known events. Videos show outcomes that never occurred. Websites shift layout upon refresh.

Reality stops behaving like a single continuous line. It becomes layered and unstable.

2.4 Phase 4 — The Truth

The subject learns that the anomalies are results of contact between multiple versions of the same identity across many realities. Rogue clones from alternate branches attempt to destroy competing branches.

Their purpose is simple: only one version can remain. Only one timeline becomes PRIME.
