PRIME

Phase 1 — Start of Story

Everything begins normally, even though small things are wrong. These differences won’t be remembered until later, once the shift begins.

Phase 2 — Strange Occurrences

Odd events appear: screams, gunshots, voices, glitches, and strange messages. Tiny pieces of information slip in, but nothing fully explains what’s happening.

Phase 3 — Breaking Point

The world stops pretending to be stable. Images, websites, channels, and messages appear nonstop, all showing conflicting versions of events. Reality feels like it’s folding in on itself.

Phase 4 — The Truth

The real issue isn’t one world breaking — it’s many worlds colliding. Rogue clones from different realities are destroying each other to become the single “prime” version. All the confusion and chaos were signs of collapsing timelines.

When the prime version is chosen, every other version ends.
